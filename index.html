What we do?

We are the sellers and distributors of pure and packaged bottled water. We are right now selling our two brands "Ken Aqua" and "Sai Garwa".

"Ken Aqua" and "Sai Garwa" are overwhelmed purified and gone through different layers of purification before serving to our customers.

Why us?

"Ken Aqua" and "Sai Garwa" follows a 10-step purification process â€“ Our water is carefully collected from different sources like underground water, rivers, harvested rainwater, etc. and is subjected to 'ozonisation', a process that is at least 50 times more powerful and 3000 times faster than chlorination in killing bacteria and other microbes

This step is followed by multiple filtration processes like sand filtration, carbon filtration and reverse osmosis that helps to get rid of unwanted particles, colour, odour and excess salts and minerals from the processed water. Filtration is then followed by Mineralization, a very essential step in the purification process where right amounts of Potassium and Magnesium compounds are added to make our water healthy.

Lastly, micron filtration and re-ozonisation helps to remove micro particles and maintain utmost hygiene in every bottle, so that we can deliver purity in every sip. Drinking ozonated water is known to increase the oxygen levels in the body, thus, helping in improving our energy levels and boosting the immune system.
